fn probe( &self, device: PciCommonDevice, ) -> Result<Arc<dyn PciDevice>, (BusProbeError, PciCommonDevice)>

Probe an unclaimed PCI device.

If the driver matches and succeeds in initializing the unclaimed device, then the driver will return an claimed instance of the device, signaling that the PCI device is now ready to work.

Once a device is matched and claimed by a driver, it won’t be fed to another driver for probing.
